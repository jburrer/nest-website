  The Nest was created as a DIY music venue for alternative and queer folks in the area. In light of recent situations, rumors surrounding the nest, and the scene as a whole, we feel like we have let down our community. As team and members of the scene, we deeply apologize to anyone who has been hurt, mistreated, or affected by the poor environment created. These situations do not reflect the morals and values of the nest. Unfortunately as a show house, we have strayed from our original intention. We also acknowledge the lack of credibility, dysfunctional power, and poor financial transparency within the nest as an organization and apologized for its effect on bands, team members, and other houses in the scene. We recognize the harm that’s been done. Continuing forward we will be providing resources, transparency, and grievance officers.
