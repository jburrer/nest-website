Finances

  For summer shows, the Nest receives an equal cut to that of the bands, which is then split between the staff working that event.

  For instance, for a show that brought in $100 with 4 bands and 4 staff, the cut will be split 5 ways, which each band getting $20 and the staff splitting the additional $20 to each get $5. For simplicity, each cut is rounded down to the nearest whole dollar, and any dollars left over remain with the Nest.
